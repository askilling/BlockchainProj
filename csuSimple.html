Project Background

This project was created in an effort to display the uses of blockchain technology. The project was put together by Dr. Kreminski, who is an Instructor at CSU-P, in an effort to introduce blockchain technology to students. Dr. Kreminski only participated in teaching an introduction to blockchain and it's uses in applications. The project itself was planned and built by Chris Toomey, Logan Vineyard, and Aaron Skilling, which made it a completely student-built project with less than 10 hours of instruction on the subject.
